Polymers' Properties Predictions

Upload a polymer image or enter its SMILES string to instantly predict:

  • Glass Transition Temperature (Tg, °C)
  • Fractional Free Volume (FFV)
  • Thermal Conductivity (κ, W/m·K)
  • Density (g/cm3)
  • Radius of Gyration (Rg, Å)

This app is powered by Flask, Celery & PyTorch, using a ResNeXt-inspired CNN for rapid property inference.


How It Works

SMILES Input: Prepend your monomer SMILES with “*” (handled automatically under the hood).
Image Input: DECIMER transformer converts images into SMILES before prediction.
